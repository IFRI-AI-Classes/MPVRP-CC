Multi-Product Vehicle Routing Problem with Changeover Cost

This site presents the MPVRP-CC, a variant of the classic vehicle routing problem applied to the distribution of petroleum products. It involves optimizing tanker truck routes that must deliver different types of fuels, taking into account the cleaning cost when a vehicle changes products.
